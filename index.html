About Agro Husk Wares.

Agro Husk Enterprise is an NGO aimed at the production of sustainable eco-friendly wares and green energy through the use of biodegradeable plant husks.
We also serve to raise awareness about climate change to ensure that everyone collectively takes part to fix our planet one husk at a time.

Our Company Here at Agro Husk Wares, we aim to bring about climate change awareness as well as play an active role in ensuring that our everyday wares are both recyclable and environmental friendly in their disposal
Safety Be at rest knowing that we at AHW care about your well being and safety as well as your comfort, through our sustainable energy.
Strategy We aim to educate the masses through awareness campaigns as well as mobile games for the younger population that helps train their mentality towards an environment-first approach to life.
Environment & Sustainability Wares at AHW are bioderadeable and environmental friendly. In their disposal, they contribute no adverse effect to the climate, rather they enrich the ecosystem for a better planet.
Green energy also produced at AHW using plant husks provide a polution free form of energy for your everyday needs.
